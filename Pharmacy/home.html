About us

Lyfe Pharmacy is a bespoke pharmacy in west didsbury, located on Lapwing Lane. We are a small team with a professional and friendly attitude, looking to give you the utmost personal care. At Lyfe pharmacy we will do everything possible to put the patient at the center of their care.
