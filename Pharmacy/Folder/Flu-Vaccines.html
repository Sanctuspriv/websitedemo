The flu can be very serious for those at risk, and can make some people fall extremely ill. If you're unsure about getting the flu vaccination this year, take into consideration the following.

  • The flu vaccine will give you the best protection against the virus
  • If you happen to catch the flu this year, the symptoms will be much more mild.
  • The vaccination will help lessen the spread of the virus, and it will help to protect those around you who might not be able to get the vaccination.
  • The vaccination will help to lessen the risk of serious complications for those who are more susceptible to catching the virus.
  • The vaccination will prevent you from being seriously ill if you happen to catch Covid-19 at the same time.
