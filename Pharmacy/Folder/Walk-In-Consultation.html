Walk-In Consultation Service

What can we help you with?

Pharmacists are qualified healthcare professionsals, and they can help assess minor illnesses and reccommend the treatment you require, whether that's over the counter medication or a bit of rest.

What can a Pharmacist help you with?

  • Sore throats
  • Coughs, Colds and Flu
  • Tummy Issues
  • Aches and Pains
  • Red, sore or itchy eyes
  • Trouble Sleeping
  • Athlete's foot
  • Mouth Ulcers
  • Constipation and Diarrhoea

Talking to a pharmacist about a minor health concern can help a condition before it gets any worse. We can help with clinical advice for a wide range of illnesses in the pharmacy, and if your symptoms suggest something more serious is going on, we can tell you what you need to do next to ensure your health and wellbeing. If asked, a pharmacist or pharmacy technician can also give you advice and guidance on how to take new medications or use inhalers. Appointments aren't required, so just visit us when you feel ready.
