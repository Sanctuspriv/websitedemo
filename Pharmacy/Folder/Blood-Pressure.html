Blood Pressure

What We Will Do

A member of our trained pharmacy team will take your blood pressure in the privacy of a consultation room. Your result will be explained to you and you will be offered any advice and support needed to manage your blood pressure. If appropriate, your results will be shared with your doctor.

Who is this service for?

All adults could possibly have high blood pressure. High blood pressure can have no symptoms, so a checkup is the best way to find out if there's an issue. While all adults can have high blood pressure, there are certain groups that have a higher risk of increased blood pressure. For example, if:

  • You're over the age of 60.
  • You have a family history of high blood pressure and/or heart disease.
  • You're overweight or don't do much exercise.
  • You're of Aisan or African-Caribbean descent.
  • You drink more than 2-3 units of alcohol a day (for women) or 3 - 4 units of alcohol a day (for men).
  • Your diet includes a lot of salt.
  • You don't eat five portions of fruit and vegetables a day.

We would be happy to discuss any blood pressure concerns you may have.


We cannot check your blood pressure if you fall into the following groups. If you fall into one of the below groups and have concerns about your blood pressure, please speak to your doctor:

  • Under 16 years of age.
  • Pregnant women.
  • Those who have had a mastectomy.
  • Those who have had their lymph glands removed.
  • Those who have fistulas.
